## Overview

This repository accompanies the manuscript:

> **Atlas of regional molecular specialization in the human brain vasculature**
We present a comprehensive single-nucleus RNA sequencing (snRNA-seq) atlas of the human brain vasculature, spanning **41 anatomically defined brain regions and major cerebral vessels** from four neurotypical middle-aged adult donors. The dataset captures **443,443 high-quality nuclei** across all major vascular and perivascular cell types, including endothelial cells, mural cells (pericytes and smooth muscle cells), fibroblasts, astrocytes, microglia, oligodendrocytes, neurons, and OPCs.
